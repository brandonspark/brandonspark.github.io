Standard ML, entirely in your browser — the code runs on
[Moscow ML compiled to WebAssembly](https://github.com/brandonspark/web-sml),
with live type checking by
[millet](https://github.com/brandonspark/millet-wasm). Toplevel responses
(`val x = 5 : int`) are shown like a REPL would; hover things to see their
types, and share your code with the Share button. Nothing leaves your
machine.
